<font size="3"> Subscription Term or Perpetual License is used via App Points (similar to tokens) in MAS.</font>

 - Contact IBM account team regarding App Points and MAS license file.
 - Customer buys a pool of App Points.
 - The same App Points can be applied to any application in MAS.

<font size="3"> There are three user types/entitlements available:  </font>

  - Limited
  - Base
  - Premium
