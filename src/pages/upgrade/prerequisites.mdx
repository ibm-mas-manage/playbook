---
title: Upgrade Prerequisites
---           

Maximo required to be at version 7612 or higher prior to MAS upgrade.

Prepare OpenShift environment(s)
- System requirements for Manage/EAM, IS, Add-ons and customizations (if any)
	- Refer to MAS and Manage documentation, determine the capacity needed and secure an OpenShift cluster for testing or development or production.
		- Red Hat OpenShift link: [OpenShift](https://docs.openshift.com/container-platform/4.5/welcome/index.html)
		- Maximo Application Suite link: [MAS](https://www.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/SSRHPA_current/appsuite/install/index.html)
		- Capacity Planning link: [Capacity Planning](https://www.ibm.com/support/pages/node/6246125)

Configure OpenShift cluster based on the above requirements.
